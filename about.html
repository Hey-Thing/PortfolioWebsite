About Me

My name is Hedin Seinen. I am 22 years old and I'm studying to become a software developer with a focus on games.

My interest in coding began with my love for games at a young age. When I was playing games on my DS, I often wondered, "How do they make this?" Years later, I found myself studying in a lab, but I quickly realized it wasn’t the right path for me. That’s when I started exploring coding and I discovered that I really enjoyed it. Since then, I’ve been studying coding and game development for about two years, and I’ve learned a lot along the way. Not only have I developed skills in creating games, but I’ve also gained experience in web development and building websites.
