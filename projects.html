LoopyLife


LoopyLife is a project I worked on during a game jam organized by Noorderpoort. We were assigned to teams of four, but our group ended up with only three people. The theme of the jam was "loop." We created a VR game where you drive to work, do your job, and drive back home repeating this cycle while trying to get as drunk as possible throughout the day. The game was developed in just one week.

Hackclusive project


Hackclusive was a commissioned project from the company Hackclusive, who wanted a game to teach children how easily hackers can access computers and steal personal data. The game was developed in just one week by a team of four, and our project was selected as the best among all participating teams.

Mansion Escape


Mansion Escape was a school project where we were tasked with creating a 2D platformer. I wanted to make mine a bit more unique by adding my own creative features. In this game, you play as Block Boy, who must escape a haunted mansion by powering up his flashlight to scare away ghosts and unlock dark doors. The game was developed in one week and was a solo project.
